Chords

Chords are the building blocks of music.They can be found through various genres of music

and being aware of how they work will help improve both for theory and in practice.

Chords are simply playing multiple notes at once, so more than one key is pushed at the same time.

There are nine types, but only one type will be mentioned here, called major chords. Major chords

are known for sounding "bright" and "happy" as opposed to minor chords that are known to sound

"gloomy" and "sad". Chords are determined by scales, which is written about below. However,

it will not be mentioned here how chords are derived from scales, but only show the major chords and their notes.

Notes Picture

B major B D# F#

Scales are a collection of related notes. They are played one note at a time in the order

given in the table below. Like chords, they are one of

the significant building blocks of music, which builds melodies and harmonies.Chords are

derived from scales. Again, just like chords, there are many types of scales, but the major

scales will only be mentioned on the table below.

Notes
